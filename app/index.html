Continental Gt

Welcome back, rock 'n' roll

England, 1960s. Young men and women flock around cafes on their stripped down motorcycles, creating a culture high on style. The #ContinentalGT is a nod to that time. The machine with a story takes one back to the time when motorcycling was all about making a statement. Watch the film to relive the cultural phenomenon that refuses to fade away.

Himalayan

Built for all roads, built for no roads

Decades of expeditions. Thousands of kilometers of traversing the most challenging terrain. Years of living and evolving with a machine that responds to the mountains. That's how we made the Himalayan, our first dual-purpose motorcycle. Here is how it was built to be tough, versatile, and adaptable to all kind of terrain.

Meteor

Crusing Through Time

Growing up in the 1980s meant that the lasting image of a motorcycle for me was a cruiser of some sort. It's not that I was a 'cruiser guy', it's just that when you see lots of something, that becomes the main frame of reference, and desire in this case! Evolving from a diet of bicycles into my late teens, I was quite mesmerised by the idea of a quintessential American style cruiser - and the first one that I actually saw in the flesh and instantly fell in love with was the Royal Enfield Citybike.
